AROUNDTHECLOCK WRAPS 2024 WITH 3 MENA DIGITAL AWARDS

December 17, 2024

Dubai, UAE � AroundtheClock Communications (ATC) has brought home three MENA Digital Awards at the MENA Digital Awards 2024 ceremony held in Dubai on December 13th, 2024.

The company submitted six entries to the MENA Digital Awards 2024, and was shortlisted for all six categories before winning three prestigious awards:

  • GOLD Best Use of Digital by Sector: Education � Bloom Education
  • GOLD Best Use of Digital by Sector: Media � MBC Dream
  • SILVER Best Integrated Media Campaign � Masdar

"We're proud to wrap 2024 with these achievements which reflect ATC's innovative and impactful digital strategies across various industries. We've competed against very powerful players in the different categories, and our wins come to reaffirm our robust capabilities and leadership in the digital marketing landscape."

� Pakinam Olama, CEO of ATC

After six years of operations in Dubai, ATC recently announced the opening of a third office in the strategically located Masdar City in Abu Dhabi as part of its expansion plan, both vertically and horizontally.
