AROUNDTHECLOCK HONORS CLIENTS AT IFTAR IN ABU DHABI

March 19, 2025

Abu Dhabi, UAE — In celebration of the Holy Month's spirit of togetherness, AroundtheClock Communications (ATC) held an Iftar for its clients at the prestigious Emirates Palace Mandarin Oriental in Abu Dhabi.

The Iftar was attended by ATC's Chairman, Mr. Rizk Naifeh, and CEO, Ms. Pakinam Olama, alongside the Abu Dhabi office team and other members of ATC, who welcomed clients from both the Abu Dhabi and the Dubai offices — including key members of Abu Dhabi Media, Masdar, Aldar and others.

"AroundtheClock has grown tremendously in a very short time thanks to the dedication of our team and the trust of our clients. With the advent of the Holy Month of Ramadan, we wanted to show our gratitude to our clients who have been part of our success and strengthen ties away from the professional setting, as fostering a family spirit is something we deeply believe in — whether within our team or with our clients."

— Rizk Naifeh, Chairman of ATC

This is the first time that AroundtheClock hosted a similar event for their clients in the Emirati capital, following the opening of their office in Masdar City in 2024. The agency plans to further engage with clients during the year through more events that celebrate fruitful partnerships.
