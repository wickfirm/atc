AROUNDTHECLOCK OPENS NEW OFFICES IN ABU DHABI

October 2024

Abu Dhabi, UAE — After six years of operations in Dubai, AroundtheClock Communications (ATC) has announced the opening of a third office in Abu Dhabi, confirming once again its firm commitment to clients in the country's capital.

Strategically located in Masdar City — named the world's most sustainable city by Cityscape Intelligence — the Abu Dhabi office marks a new milestone in ATC's journey and falls within the company's expansion plan, both vertically and horizontally.

Since the company's establishment in 2018, ATC has built a solid reputation in the local market, collaborating with well-respected clients including COP28, Masdar, Zayed Sustainability Prize, Abu Dhabi Islamic Bank, and Bassam Freiha Art Foundation, among others.

"We strive to be a leading name in the advertising, communications and media industry and a pioneer in client servicing, always going the extra mile and working hard to meet clients' needs. We're aiming for a 30% growth in clients by acquiring new accounts in Abu Dhabi, notably in key industries like Real Estate, Automotive, Sustainability, and the public sector."

— Rizk Naifeh, CEO and Co-Founder of ATC

ATC won the COP28 account, which features as a remarkable achievement and a major success story in the company's proven track record. With Abu Dhabi now added to Dubai and Beirut, ATC continues to build its regional footprint as one of the most dynamic integrated communications companies in the MENA region.
